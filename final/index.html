Remembering Kohen Diego Williams

On October 19, 2008, we gave Kohen back to God. We miss him every minute of every day. This park symbolizes the joy Kohen brought us, the determination he taught us and our gratitude to God for the time he gave us with Kohen. If you feel just a fraction of that joy as you play here with your family, this park will have served its purpose. From our family to yours, Kohen's mark upon the world...KOHEN'S PARK.

Missions

Mission work is at the heart of Kohen's Park. We want everyone to make it to heaven and know Jesus the way that Kohen does now. Through our BEYOND BORDERS CONCERT and MEMORIAL LANTERNS, we are able to contribute to multiple mission trips around the world and help to build churches in India.

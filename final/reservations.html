Reservations

Our pavilion is available for CHILDREN'S BIRTHDAY PARTIES on Saturday from 10-12 or 12:30-2:30 and Sunday from 1:00-3:00. TEAMS MAY BOOK PARTIES ON WEEKNIGHTS FROM 5:30-7:30 CALL WITH QUESTIONS. 765-0135

There is a $75.00 reservation fee which is payable if the reservation request is granted. In order to keep Kohen's Park clean and safe for all visitors we have established the following rules. PLEASE read these before you submit your request. We do not make any exceptions to the rules.

Rules:

1. NO SMOKING, PETS, PINATAS, OR CHARCOAL GRILLS ARE ALLOWED IN THE PARK OR PARKING LOT. PLEASE notify your guests about the rules regarding smoking and pets when you distribute the invitations.

2. NO DECORATIONS CAN BE HUNG ON OUR FENCE, PAVILION OR TREES.

3. NO TAPE CAN BE USED ON OUR PAVILION OR TABLES.

4. FOOD AND DRINKS MUST BE KEPT IN THE PAVILION AREA AND THE GATE MUST BE KEPT CLOSED.

5. THE RESERVATION IS FOR THE TABLES UNDER THE PAVILION ONLY. The 4 tables will seat approximately 16 children and 16 adults. Please remember the rest of the park remains open to the public.

WE REGULARLY MONITOR PARTIES TO ENSURE ALL RULES ARE BEING FOLLOWED.
